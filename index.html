Why Choose UFL?

Since 1971 the University of Florida Department of Health Education and Behavior has offered a Master of Science in Health Education and Behavior.

To meet the worldwide demand for health education specialists, which is growing at a rate of 13% over the next 10 years (faster than the average for all occupations)*, the University of Florida now offers a fully online master's program.

This fully online program offers a flexible schedule, perfect for working professionals, and offers worldwide accessibility.
